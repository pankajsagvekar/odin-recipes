Kanda Bhaji

Description

Onion bhaji is a popular street food snack in Maharashtra. There are many variations to preparing them. I am sharing here my momâ€™s recipe which she has been making for many years now.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups thinly sliced onions
  • 1/4 cups besan
  • 1/2 tbsp coarsely crushed whole coriander (dhania) seeds
  • 2 tsp finely chopped green chillies
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder (haldi)
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped coriander (dhania)
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. To make kanda bhaji, combine all the ingredients along with 2 tbsp of water in a deep bowl and mix well using your hands.

  2. Heat the oil in a deep non-stick kadhai and drop spoonfuls of the onion mixture using your fingers, few at a time and deep-fry till they turn golden brown in colour from all the sides. Drain on an absorbent paper and keep aside.

  3. Serve kanda bhaji immediately.
